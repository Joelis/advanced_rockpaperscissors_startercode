Rock Paper Scissors Game

Rock Paper Scissors is a hand game played, typically, between two people, that requires luck and basic psychology. The two players show a hand gesture (rock, paper, or scissors) and depending on the gestures each displays, one person wins. Rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, and paper beats rock. "Rock" is isplayed as a closed fist, "Paper" is an open and flat hand, and "Scissors" is the index and middle finger extended into a "V" formation. If both players show the same gesture then it results in a tie.
